Air conditioning safety helmet:
This project innovatively combines comfort and safety by integrating a miniature air conditioning unit into a lightweight helmet. Designed for workers in hot environments, it provides continuous cool air, reducing heat stress and enhancing well-being. This project aims to improve productivity and health in demanding work conditions.

Idea of implementation:
This project is based on using a 555 timer ic combined with nand gate and a nor gate with a transistor to display 4 words consequently with a period of 1 second between them (we also used a var resistance to make this period adjustable) , these 4 words as shown in the video are ASU ENG CSE FUN .

Idea of LDR working:
light detecting resistor is a passive transducers that changes resistance depending on the amount of light incident on it and operates on semiconductor photoconductivity; the energy of photons hitting the semiconductor frees electrons to flow, decreasing the resistance.
Project idea:
We connected this ldr to a BC547 transistor to revert its function so when sunlight falls on it the connected leds will turn off to decrease loss of energy.
